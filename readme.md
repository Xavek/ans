## What is ANS?

ANS is a naming system on Starknet that breaks free from the traditional TLD (top-level domain) monopoly. While other naming systems lock you into `.eth`, `.stark`, or `.sol`, ANS lets you mint **anything you want** (with some ground rules).

## Features

**🎨 True Freedom**
- Mint `.pizza`, `.moon`, `.wagmi`, or `.probably-nothing`
- Your imagination is the limit (terms and conditions apply*)

**🚫 Respect Other Ecosystems**
- Can't mint `.stark`, `.eth`, `.sol`, or other established naming systems
- If you want those, go to their respective platforms
- We play nice with the neighbors

**💰 Community-First Economics with Revenue Share**
- Mint fees start as low as **zero**
- Suffix admins now earn revenue share from their TLDs
- Portion of fees flows to suffix administrators
- Remainder splits between protocol fee receiver and Vesu earn pools

## How It Works

1. Choose your unique name (avoid restricted TLDs)
2. Mint it on-chain for minimal fees
3. Hold your name, earn from protocol yield (eventually)
4. Flex your creative naming prowess

## Economic Flow

```
Mint Fee (≥ 0)
    ├─→ Suffix Admin Revenue Share (configurable)
    ├─→ Protocol Fee Receiver
    └─→ Vesu Earn Pools
            └─→ Yield → Claim directly on Vesu
```

## Restricted TLDs

The following are off-limits (go to their respective platforms):
- `.stark` - Use Starknet naming services
- `.eth` - Use ENS
- `.sol` - Use Solana naming services
- Other ecosystem-specific names
